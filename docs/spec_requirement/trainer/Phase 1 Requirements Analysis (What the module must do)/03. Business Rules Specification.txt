Purpose: Defines trainer management and availability business rules.

Business Rules:

BR-01: Trainer name is mandatory and cannot be empty.
BR-02: Email address must be unique, non-empty, and valid format.
BR-03: Phone number must be unique, non-empty, and contain exactly 10 digits.
BR-04: Experience must be a non-negative integer (>= 0).
BR-05: Trainer status must be 'Active' or 'Inactive'.
BR-06: Dynamic Date-Specific Availability Resolution Hierarchy:
   Availability for any trainer on a given date resolves in the following order:
   1. Trainer Override: If a record exists in `trainer_availablity` for the trainer and date, resolve to its status (1 = Available, 0 = Unavailable).
   2. Calendar Event: If a global event exists in `calendar_event` on that date (Public Holiday or Company Meeting), resolve to Unavailable (0).
   3. Weekends: If the date is Saturday or Sunday, resolve to Unavailable (0).
   4. Default: Resolve to Available (1).
BR-07: Audit Tracing:
   All database writes and updates to the trainer, availability, and calendar tables must record user audit metadata via `created_by` and `updated_by` fields.
BR-08: Automatic Holidays:
   Holidays for the India region should be auto-populated for calendar years to prevent manual registration overhead.
