Purpose
Defines trainer management business rules.
The Business rules should be followed accurately 


Business Rules
BR-01 → Trainer name is mandatory.

BR-02 → Email must be unique.

BR-03 → Phone number must be valid and unique.

BR-04 → Experience cannot be negative.

BR-05 → Trainer status must be Active or Inactive.

BR-06 → Unavailable trainers cannot be assigned.

BR-07 → Deleted trainers cannot be restored without re-registration.

BR-008 → Administrator performs all trainer operations.
